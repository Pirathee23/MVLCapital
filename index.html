About Us

Who we are and what we do.

Est. 2021

Real Estate Investment Firm

MVL Capital

MVL Capital is a real estate investment company that invests smart capital and provides practical solutions for real estate opportunities. MVL Capital has expertise in buying and managing real estate, especially properties where they can provide added value.

We aim to consistently deliver best-in-class returns to investors by paring opportunistic acquisitions with determined execution. MVL Capital investment performance is a result of a solutions-oriented approach to property acquisition, development, management, and divestiture. MVL Capital will continue to produce exceptional returns and services for its investors by working with partners that share the same entrepreneurial drive, operational precision, and extraordinary performance goals.

Meet the Members of the Real Estate Investment Firm.

Meet the Co-Founder.

Mathusan Selvarasa.

Mathusan is the president and co-founder of MVL Capital, a Toronto-based real estate investment firm. Mr. Selvarasa began investing in real estate and the money market at the age of 17 and has since amassed a substantial investment portfolio. With over 5 years of industry experience, he plans to expand MVL Capital into a multi-million-dollar organization in the next few years.

Mathusan has a demonstrated track record of launching profitable businesses and systems, one of which is a Toronto-based IT consulting firm specializing in data analytics. Mathusan is currently focusing solely on real estate, intending to become an accredited real estate agent to better serve his clients. Strategic thinking, planning, execution, negotiation, and relationship building are among his specialties.

Are you interested in becoming a future MVL Capital investor? Or perhaps you'd want to follow the progress of MVL Capital and Mathusan? Follow @MVLCapital & @Mathusan1997 to join the journey.

Meet the Co-Founder.

Vaishnavy Ravindiran.

Vaishnavy Ravindiran is the founding partner of MVL Capital, a Toronto-based real estate investment firm. Ms. Ravindiran blends her enthusiasm for real estate with her commitment to education and the development of strong client relationship building. She is now pursuing to become a Mortgage Advisor to assist potential investors and further scale the business.

Vaishnavy earned a Bachelor of Commerce from the Ted Rogers School of Management, with a focus on real estate and law, before entering the real estate industry in 2019. The book "Rich Dad, Poor Dad" sparked Vaishnavy's curiosity and desire for starting this business. She connects with her clients and provides recommendations on how to become a market expert and provides above-and-beyond services.
