About Us

Who we are and what we do.

Est. 2021

Real Estate Investment Firm

MVL Capital

MVL Capital is a real estate investment company that invests smart capital and provides practical solutions for real estate opportunities. MVL Capital has expertise in buying and managing real estate, especially properties where they can provide added value.

We aim to consistently deliver best-in-class returns to investors by paring opportunistic acquisitions with determined execution. MVL Capital investment performance is a result of a solutions-oriented approach to property acquisition, development, management, and divestiture. MVL Capital will continue to produce exceptional returns and services for its investors by working with partners that share the same entrepreneurial drive, operational precision, and extraordinary performance goals.

Years of Experience

    Mathusan is a natural-born entrepreneur as he had many successes and failures with his startups. His most recent venture is MVL Capital which he co-founded with his partner Vaishnavy. Mathusan is now looking to take his investment portfolio to the next level by poking into the real estate market. Mathusan carried his method into every detail of life and lived on his earnings with extreme frugality. He graduated from Business Technology Management at Ted Rogers School of Management with $0 debt as his investment vehicles paid it for him. He comes with a strong background in IT and business which was acquired from his multiple internships and schooling. Currently, Mathusan is pursuing his professional career by working as a consultant for a large IT MSP. Mathusan is passionate about investing and dreams to soon reach his goal of financial independence. He started investing in the stock market at the age of 18 and dived into the real estate market by purchasing 2 very high in demand pre-construction developments in the Greater Toronto Area and is looking to make his footprint in the real estate market.
